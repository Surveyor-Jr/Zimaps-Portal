About

ZiMaps portal is a place built by Zimbabweans for Zimbabweans to enable the searching & retrieval of any digital maps created by the community for public usage. Here one can find maps in both digital & downloadable format. It also acts as a place for spatial freelancers to showcase their work & get hired.
